How to Play

Your Goal:

Guess the unkown pro cyclist.

Each guess reveals that cyclist's attributes and compares them to the attributes of the unkown cyclist.

Directions

1

Search for a cyclist to make your first guess.

2

Narrow down the peleton with each guess.

3

Piece together the clues until you guess the cyclist.

Difficulty

Difficulty is based on the number and popularity of cyclists in the game.

Noob

20 of the most famous pro cyclists today.

Easy

About 200 of the most well-known cyclists.

Medium

About half of the cyclists in the game.

Hard

The entire list. Roughly 1000 cyclists.

The full breakdown of which cyclists are in the list is available under the attributes tab.

Which proffesional cyclists are in the game?

The complete list of cyclists includes:

● Every cyclists from all 18 mens UCI World Tour teams and all 14 womens UCI World Tour teams.

● Every cyclists from the following mens UCI ProTeams: Tudor Pro Cycling, Cofidis, Pinarello Q36.5 Pro Cycling, and Unibet Rose Rockets.

● Significant pro cyclists who retired after 2020.

● A few additions on UCI ProTeams that were not included: Jesús Herrada, Fernando Gaviria, Iván Ramiro Sosa, Yukiya Arashiro, Anthony Turgis, Allison Jackson, and Chris Froome (Not retired but not on a team).

The Daily ProCycle will reset to the full list at the start of ever month.
